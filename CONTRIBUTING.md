## Contributor License Agreement

Before we can accept your contribution, you need to sign our [CLA](CLA.md).

### How to Sign

**Using CLA Assistant (automated on GitHub):**
When you submit your first pull request, CLA Assistant will automatically 
comment asking you to sign. Just follow the link and sign with your GitHub account.

**Manual signing:**
If you prefer, you can sign manually by adding to your first PR your details to 
[CONTRIBUTORS.md](CONTRIBUTORS.md).
